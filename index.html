The Crystal Collector

Count Your Crystals. . .

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crysal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above your random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each crystals value will change anew randomly at the start of each new game. Good luck!
